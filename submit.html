Submission Guidelines

Exact quotes only. We need the actual words, not a summary. Paraphrasing changes the logic.

Public figures, public statements. We analyze rhetoric from politicians, pundits, and public officials speaking in their public capacity.

Context matters. The same sentence can be a fallacy or a fair play depending on what came before and after it.

Both sides welcome. We're nonpartisan. Submit quotes from any party, any ideology. We call the logic, not the politics.

Fair plays too! Spotted a politician making a genuinely well-reasoned argument? Those are rarer than you'd think, and we love finding them.
